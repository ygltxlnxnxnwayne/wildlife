THE LION (KING OF THE JUNGLE)

The lion is a large cat of the genus panthera native to Africa and India.
It has a muscular, deep-chested body, short,rounded head, round ears and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
It is sexually dimorphic; adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane.
It is a social species, forming groups called prides.
A lion's pride consists of a few adult males, realated females and cubs.
Groups of female lions usually hunt together, preying mostly on ungulates.
The lion is an apex and keystone predator; although some lions scavenge when opportunities occur.
